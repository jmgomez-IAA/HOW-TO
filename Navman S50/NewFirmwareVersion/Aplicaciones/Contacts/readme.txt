PPC Customer Manager  for Pocket PC 2.0
============================================================================

PPC Customer Manager for Pocket PC, a business utility for organizing customer 
infomation is now available from our web site.

PPC Customer Manager is an easy-to-use business information organizer.
You can use it to keep track of your customers' information like 
company address, phone and fax number, website address and resell number.
It can handle mutiple contact person's information of each customer. 
You can write notes for each customer, define your own "user defined fields", 
search for a customer, export customer list to a text file.
