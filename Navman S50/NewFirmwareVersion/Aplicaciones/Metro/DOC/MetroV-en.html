Depending on circumstances, additional files are provided, in case of important works disrupting the public transportation networks (e.g. in Berlin, New York & Paris) or for exceptional services set up for special events (e.g. New Year's eve or sport events). These files may or may not be included in our regular distribution packages but, in any case, you will find all information on our blog.

Unavailable in our standard packages, a specific file is also provided for the subway network of Liberty City (from the Grand Theft Auto IV game).
